4. tuliskan 5 (lima) profesi ke dalam bahasa ingris !​


Tuliskan 5 (lima) profesi ke dalam bahasa Inggris !

Pendahuluan :

Profesi adalah sinonim [ persamaan kata ] dari pekerjaan. Sedangkan pekerjaan adalah kegiatan sehari-hari yang dilakukan untuk mencukupi kebutuhan hidup manusia. Pada kesempatan kali ini, kita akan menyebutkan contoh pekerjaan dalam bahasa Inggris.

Penjelasan :

Berikut ini daftar profesi atau pekerjaan dan bahasa Inggrisnya :

1. Dokter ⇒ doctor

2. Guru ⇒ teacher

3. Suster ⇒ nurse

4. Sopir ⇒ driver

5. Penyanyi ⇒ singer

6. Petani ⇒ farmer

7. Nelayan ⇒ fisherman

8. Pengusaha ⇒ businessman

9. Pilot ⇒ pilot

10. Pelaut ⇒ sailor

11. Penjahit ⇒ tailor

12. Penari ⇒ dancer

13. Pelukis ⇒ painter

14. Pesenam ⇒ gymnast

etc

5. percakapan kelas bersih bahasa ingris​


Jawaban:

Berikut ini adalah contoh percakapan kelas bersih dalam bahasa Inggris:

Siswa A: Good morning, everyone! I hope you all had a great weekend.

Siswa B: Good morning! Yes, I had a relaxing weekend. How about you?

Siswa A: Oh, I had a productive weekend. I finished my homework and did some reading.

Siswa C: That's great! I wish I could be that disciplined.

Siswa D: Don't worry, I'm sure you'll get there. Let's motivate each other to stay focused.

Guru: Good morning, class! I see everyone is in a good mood today. That's excellent. Let's start our lesson.

Siswa E: Good morning, Teacher! We're ready to learn.

Guru: Great! Today, we're going to discuss a new topic in Math. It might be a bit challenging, but I believe we can handle it.

Siswa F: That sounds intriguing, Teacher! I'm excited to learn new concepts.

Siswa G: Excuse me, Teacher. Could you please clarify one thing from our previous lesson?

Guru: Of course, Siswa G. I'm here to clarify any confusion you may have. Please go ahead and ask your question.

Siswa G: Thank you, Teacher. I've been struggling with understanding quadratic equations. Could you explain them again?

Guru: Absolutely. Let's take some time to revisit quadratic equations so that everyone is on the same page.

Siswa H: I appreciate that, Siswa G. I also had some questions about that topic.

Siswa I: Teacher, may I ask a question unrelated to the lesson?

Guru: Of course, Siswa I. What's on your mind?

Siswa I: I wanted to suggest organizing a class outing during the next long weekend. What does everyone think?

Siswa J: That sounds like a fantastic idea! It would be a great way to bond as a class.

Guru: I appreciate your suggestion, Siswa I. Let's discuss it further after we wrap up today's lesson.

Siswa K: Teacher, before we end the class, I just wanted to say that I really enjoyed today's discussion.

Guru: Thank you, Siswa K. I'm glad to hear that. Remember, learning is a collaborative journey, and your enthusiasm motivates me as well.

Guru: As our class comes to an end, I want to remind everyone to keep practicing good study habits and maintaining a clean learning environment.

Siswa L: We will, Teacher. Thank you for the reminder.

Guru: You're welcome, Siswa L. Have a great day, everyone!

Catatan: Pastikan dalam percakapan kelas, bahasa yang digunakan tetap sopan dan terjaga kesopanannya. Gunakan frasa yang sesuai dengan situasi dan latihan Bahasa Inggris untuk memperkaya kosakata kalian.
