12. Bahasa inggris nya : Maaf aku lama balas pesan dari kamu


kelas : SMP
Pelajaran : Bahasa Inggris
Kategori : aplogize expressions
kata kunci : balas, pesan

pembahasan:
Maaf aku lama balas pesan dari kamu, merupakan kalimat yang tidak baku dalam bahasa Indonesia.
Bila untuk meminta maaf dalam membalas pesan, dapat menggunakan bahasa Indonesia: Maaf, saya tidak segera membalas pesan kamu (I'm sorry. I can't reply your message soon.

contoh lainnya dalam bentuk informal:
sorry for late reply (maaf untuk balasan yang terlambat)

22. bahasa inggris nya " kenapa kamu lama balas pesan aku?" kamu kemana aja?"


The phrase above is usually uttered from one annoyed party to another like between friends, people who are in a relationship or boss to his/her subordinate. The proper english version of such phrase is

'What took you so long to reply me?', 'Where have you been?'

Penjelasan:

Translating phrase or words from English to Indonesian or vice verse, or even to any language, one has to consider these stuffs to avoid any possible confusion for the native speakers, such as

context

proper vocabulary

The word-by-word translation is not recommended as it can be potentially confusing. For example if such literal translation it becomes like this:

'why you long to reply my message? ' you where just?'

Such phrase or expression is grammatically wrong. So proper one should be taken from the context. One normally says that as he/she's annoyed by the fact that she/he didn't get the reply instantly. So, then one said it and asked where did they go or if the speaker's partner just shows up and see the speaker directly, one would say 'where have you been?' in English context. So the proper translated version of such phrase is

'What took you so long to reply me? (not 'why were you too long' as such phrase does not exist in English daily conversation.)

and 'Where have you been?' (not 'where were you?' because it sounds more like asking about the place he went to in the past or in Indonesian 'kamu kemana kemarin?' not 'kamu kemana saja?'
