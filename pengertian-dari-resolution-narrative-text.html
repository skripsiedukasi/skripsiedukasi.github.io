 The Smart Monkey And The Dull Crocodile
One day there was a monkey. He wanted to cross a river. There he saw a crocodile so he asked the crocodile to take him across the other side of the river. The crocodile agree and told the monkey to jump on its back. Then the crocodile swam down the river with the monkey on his top.
Unluckily, the crocodile was very hungry, he stopped in the middle of the river and said to the monkey, “My father is very sick. He has to eat the heart of the monkey. So he will be healthy again.”
At the time, the monkey was in dangerous situation and he had to think hard. Then he had a good idea. He told the crocodile to swim back to the river bank. “What’s for?” asked the crocodile. “Because I don’t bring my heart,” said the monkey. “I left it under a tree, near some coconuts in the river bank.”
The crocodile agreed and turned around. He swam back to the bank of the river. As soon as they reached the river bank, the monkey jumped off the crocodile’s back. Then he climbed up to the top of a tree.
“Where is your heart?” asked the crocodile. “You are foolish,” said the monkey to the crocodile. “Now I am free and I have my heart.”


Orientation : The participants or characters of the story are a smart monkey and dull crocodile.

complication : Every narrative text must consist of conflict or problem. A simple definition of problem is when something goes and it is not what we want. In the story the complication start when the crocodile want to eats the monkey.
the crocodile was very hungry, he stopped in the middle of the river and said to the monkey, “My father is very sick. He has to eat the heart of the monkey. Of course the monkey don not want to be the crocodile’s meal and that is the problem which sets the whole story.

Resolution : A problem must be resolved. It can succeed or fail. In this story, the monkey succeeds to solve the problem. He get free from the hungry crocodile.

7. orientation,complication,resolution,and coda dari narrative text finding farmers magic box ​


Struktur tek pada cerita Finding Farmers Magic Box:

Orientation:

In the afternoon, there was a poor farmer. He lives with his wife.

Complication:

Apple began to disappear and the box full of gold coins. The farmer soon became rich.

Resolution:

As well as gold coins began to disappear and the box is filled with the dead. The dead bodies should be pulled out and buried. To do this, the farmer had to spend all the money that had been collected earlier.

Coda

When he had spent all his money, broke the box. The farmer was ugly as before, terrible ending.

Pembahasan

Terdapat 4 struktur dalam teks narasi, antara lain orientasi, komplikasi, resolusi, dan reorientasi:

1. Orientasi: Penulis membuat kalimat yang menjabarkan latar waktu, tempat, penjelasan tokoh dan wataknya. Teks narasi menceritakan sudut pandang setiap tokoh.

2. Komplikasi: Dalam teks narasi ada peristiwa atau kejadian penting. Peristiwa ini diceritakan secara detail mulai dari penyebab, pemicu, sampai timbulnya konflik antar tokoh. Konflik ini menimbulkan peristiwa lain hingga mencapai puncaknya.

3. Resolusi: Struktur ketiga menceritakan konflik cerita yang semakin menurun. Kemudian konflik bisa diselesaikan.

4. Reorientasi adalah struktur keempat adalah penutup teks. Dalam teks narasi, penutup menyampaikan pesan moral dari cerita tersebut. Namun, reorientasi atau disebut juga coda sifatnya opsional bisa tidak dihadirkan dalam teks.

9. narrative text usually consist of three parts; orientation, complication, resolution, explain them​


Jawaban:

1) Orientation

Sets the scene: where and when the story happened and introduces the participants of the story: who and what is involved in the story.

2) Complication

Tells the beginning of the problems which leads to the crisis (climax) of the main participants.

3) Resolution

The problem (the crisis) is resolved, either in a happy ending or in a sad (tragic) ending

4) Re-orientation/Coda

This is a closing remark to the story and it is optional. It consists of a moral lesson, advice or teaching from the writer

the generic structure narrative text

1. orientation: characters, time, setting

2. complication: confict/problem

3. resolution: how the problem is solved

14. Pengertian narrative text dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Narrative text is a kind of text that contains imagination or engineered true story or legends

PENJELASAN

NARRATIVE TEXT

Narrative text is a text presents the myths, fables, novels, and other realistic fiction stories used for entertaining the readers. Besides, the moral values can be taken .

Social Function/ Communicative Purpose  :

to amuse/entertain the readers

The main Generic structure of the Narrative text :

1. Orientation

=> The first paragraph presents the story plot ( background ) include where the story takes a place, when the story happens, and who are involved in the story.

2. Complication  

=> the further paragraph, depicts the occurred and developed problem . The problem will be more complicated based on the story given. This complication is created to evoke the reader’s mood. It is the main part of the fiction either drama or narrative.  

3. Resolution  

=> This paragraph gives the conclusion from whole of the ending story whether happy or sad and even give the suspended summary based on the readers's point of view.

4. Coda

=> This part contains the moral values/message which is taken from the concluded story

Language feature of the narrative text

1. The using of Simple past tense  

Since the unreal situation/fake story, the simple past tense structure in the text is emphasized.

2. The using of the direct speech

In the narrative text, the conversation is often used to support the story.

3. The using of specific time related to the past  

At the beginning of the paragraph, no wonder the author  uses the time signal “Once upon a time................”  because it is the main typical of the narrative text.

16. ka buatin narrative text dalam bahasa inggrisdan analisisstruktur-orientation-complication-resolution-reorientation​


Jawaban:

Title: A Day at the Amusement Park

Orientation:

Once upon a time in the sunny town of Happyville, there was a family consisting of Dad, Mom, and their two children, Tim and Sarah. They had been planning a trip to the local amusement park for weeks, and the day had finally arrived. Excitement filled the air as they piled into their car and set off on their adventure.

Complication:

As they arrived at the amusement park, they were greeted by a massive crowd of people. The entrance lines were long, and the kids couldn't contain their restlessness. To make matters worse, the park's star attraction, a colossal roller coaster, had broken down due to technical issues. This was a significant disappointment for both Tim and Sarah, as they had been looking forward to it the most.

Resolution:

Despite the initial setbacks, the family decided to make the most of their day at the amusement park. They explored various rides, enjoyed cotton candy, and laughed their hearts out in the funhouse. The roller coaster finally reopened later in the day, and both kids had a thrilling ride. They took memorable photos and shared quality time with their parents.

Reorientation:

As the day came to a close, the family was pleasantly surprised to find that their day at the amusement park, despite the hiccups, had turned into a fantastic adventure filled with laughter and joy. They learned that sometimes, unexpected challenges can lead to memorable experiences, and it's the company of loved ones that truly matters. Happyville's amusement park became a place they would cherish forever.

Analysis of the Narrative Structure:

- Orientation: The story introduces the main characters and sets the scene by describing their plan to visit the amusement park.

- Complication: The conflict arises when they encounter the long lines and the broken roller coaster, creating disappointment and frustration.

- Resolution: The family decides to make the most of their day, exploring other attractions and enjoying each other's company. The roller coaster eventually opens, providing a happy ending to their day.

- Reorientation: The story concludes by highlighting the unexpected joy they experienced, emphasizing the value of family and the lessons learned during their visit to the amusement park.

Title: Lost in the Woods

Once upon a time, there was a young girl named Emily who loved exploring the woods near her home. One sunny afternoon, she decided to venture deeper into the woods than she had ever gone before. She packed some snacks, put on her sturdy boots, and set off on her adventure.

Orientation:

Emily was an adventurous girl who loved spending time in the woods. She had explored the woods near her home many times before but had never gone too far.

Complication:

As Emily wandered deeper into the woods, she realized that the path she was following had become overgrown and difficult to navigate. She tried retracing her steps, but she soon realized that she had lost her way. Panic set in as she realized she was alone, with no idea how to get back home.

Resolution:

Emily took a deep breath and tried to stay calm. She remembered some basic survival skills she had learned from her outdoor camping trips with her family. She found a clearing and decided to stay put, building a makeshift shelter using branches and leaves to protect herself from the cold night.

She also used her whistle to call for help, hoping that someone would hear her. She rationed her snacks and drank water from a nearby stream to stay hydrated. Emily kept her spirits up by talking to herself and singing songs to pass the time.

Re-orientation/coda:

After spending two nights in the woods, Emily was finally rescued by a search and rescue team. She was overjoyed to see familiar faces and was grateful for their help. She learned from her experience to always be prepared and never venture too far without proper navigation tools.

From that day on, Emily continued to enjoy her adventures in the woods, but she made sure to bring a compass, map, and whistle with her at all times. She also took a wilderness survival course to further enhance her skills. Emily's experience in the woods had taught her valuable lessons about the importance of preparedness and safety in outdoor adventures.

Generic structure of Narrative Text:

Orientation: Introducing the main character and setting the scene for the story.Complication: Presenting the problem or challenge that the main character faces.Resolution: Describing how the main character overcomes the challenge and resolves the problem.Re-orientation/coda: Concluding the story and providing a moral or reflection on the experience.

33. generic structure of narrative text Nyi Roro Kidul : 1. orientation ...... 2. complication .... 3. resolution ..... 4. Re-orientation .....


Jawaban:

Orientation

Orientation merupakan paragraf pembuka teks yang mengenalkan siapa tokoh yang terlibat dalam kejadian (who) serta latar waktu (when) dan tempat yang melatarbelakangi kejadian (where). Orientation ini terletak di paragraf pertama narrative text. Jadi jangan lupa dengan who, when, dan where dalam orientation pada narrative text, ya!

Complication

Struktur selanjutnya yaitu complication. Complication terletak setelah orientation selesai. Complication terdiri dari paragraf yang menjelaskan awal masalah. Awal masalah ini juga mengawali rentetan plot (alur cerita) yang kemudian akan berlanjut pada konflik, klimaks, dan anti klimaks cerita. Umumnya complication berisi interaksi antarpelaku peristiwa. Interaksi ini yang menimbulkan sebuah konflik atau pertentangan. Ada 3 jenis konflik yang mungkin terjadi di complication, yaitu natural conflict, social conflict, dan psychological conflict. Sesuai dengan namanya, natural conflict adalah permasalahan antara pelaku dan alam, social conflict adalah permasalahan antar pelaku, sedangkan psychological conflict adalah permasalahan pelaku dengan dirinya sendiri.  

Resolution

Resolution adalah paragraf yang menjadi akhir cerita. Dalam bagian ini, terjadi penyelesaian yang membuat jalan cerita berakhir. Setiap permasalahan yang muncul harus ada penyelesaiannya, bisa ditutup dengan akhir yang menyenangkan (happy ending) atau berakhir tragis atau menyedihkan (sad ending).

Reorientation

Reorientation merupakan kalimat penutup yang menceritakan kondisi terakhir sang tokoh dalam cerita atau pesan moral cerita. Oh iya, bagian ini tidak wajib ada pada narrative text, ya!
