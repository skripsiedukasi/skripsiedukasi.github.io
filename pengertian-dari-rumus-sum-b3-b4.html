dengan (=), termasuk sum.

18. jika A1 = 85° maka tentukanlah: • besar B3 • besar B4 ​


Jawaban:

Besar B3 = 85°

Besar B4 = 95°

----------------

Pembahasan

A1 = B1 = A3 = B3

A4 = B4 = A2 = B2

1. Besar B3

A1 = B3

85° = 85°

B3 = 85°

2. Besar B4

B4 + B3 = 180°

B4 + 85° = 180°

B4 = 180 - 85

B4 = 95°
