28. Contoh teks karangan boarding school


Student who reside in school premises under supervision of school teacher's are usually consider as boarding school. Here student stay away from their family members and relatives. Few people tells that it is beneficial for student and some have opposite view of it. Though this is a debatable issue and it has similar pros and cons for students. But i contend that boarding school is rational for student life since it brings lots of opportunity.

Firstly, A student got self dependence who live in boarding school. From early age he used to do his own work by himself. A student learns how to take decision in different circumstance. For example: In boarding school a student falls in numerous difficult situation where he can pull him up only by himself. He should have achieve that capability to overcome those situation. Those who live with their family most of the time they do not do their own work without other help like: washing own cloths, wrap up own sleeping bed. These unique characteristics makes a student more viable.

Secondly, Student becomes more social person while he lives in boarding school compare to those who stay at their home. Ordinarily, boarding school students need to collaborate with different types of person from diverse ages group. Which build him as a social person. For Instance, In school hostel a student have to share his room with others, which teach him to grow up with sharing mentality. He earns the ability to communicate with other student independently without any hesitation.

At last, we stretch out that boarding school prepare a social,self dependent and inquisitive person to survive in this challenging world
