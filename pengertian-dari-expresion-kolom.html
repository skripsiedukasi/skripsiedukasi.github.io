1. PENGERTIAN COMMAND EXPRESION DAN PHOHIBITION EXPRESION ?


kategori : Bahasa Inggris - Expressions
kelas : SMA

pembahasan:
Command expression is an expression which asks you to do something. (ekspresi memberi perintah adalah sebuah ekspresi yang memintamu untuk melakukan sesuatu)
examples : Open your book!
Turn off the lamps!
Pull the door, please!

Prohibition expression is an expression which bans or prohibits you to do something. (ekspresi melarang adalah sebuah ekspresi yang melarangmu untuk melakukan sesuatu)
examples:
don't come home late! or i won't open the door!
no smoking here!
don't throw your garbages at there!

25. Sugestion and expresion​


Jawaban:

1. Conversation 1 merupakan ungkapan saran, dan conversation 2 adalah tawaran.

2. Conversation 1:

• Making suggestion (memberi/menanyakan saran):

"nothing much,would you like to go to movies?"

"how about in the afternoon?"

"let's watch "ring!"

"how about "peanuts"?"

• Accepting suggestion (menyetujui saran):

"great! what movie do you want to watch?"

"ok let's go! when do you want to go?"

"it sounds good, ok see you then"

• Declining suggestion (menolak saran):

"i would love to, but not right now"

"i would rather not. i am not into horror movies"

conversation 2:

• Making offer (menawarkan sesuatu):

"would you like any help?"

"tell me what i can do and i start right away"

• Accepting offer (menerima tawaran):

"yes please, i would really appreciate it"

• Declining offer:

39. Mention two expresion of suggestion


Two expression of suggestions:You should visit your grandparents, they miss you very much.You shouldn’t leave your phone here, this area is not safe.

PembahasanExpression of Suggestion

➡️ Asking Suggestion adalah ungkapan berupa menanyakan berupa saran.

What should I do?Can you tell me an advice?Will you give me some suggestions?Do you have any idea?

➡️ Giving Suggestions adalah ungkapan dalam memberikan saran.

You should...You should not...You had better...I suggest you...

Kesimpulan

1. You should visit your grandparents, they miss you very much.

Kamu harus mengunjungi kakek-nenekmu, mereka sangat merindukanmu.Kalimat ini berisi sugesti atau saran untuk mengunjungi kakek-nenek

2. You shouldn’t leave your phone here, this area is not safe.

Kamu tidak boleh meninggalkan hpmu di sini, area ini tidak aman.Kalimat ini berisi sugesti atau saran untuk tidak meletakkan hp sembarangan
