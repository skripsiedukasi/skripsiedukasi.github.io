23. then,pour the seasoning, vegetable oil and chili powder on a bowl while cooking the noodles Ubah ke pasif


PASSIVE CONSTRUCTION FOR IMPERATIVE SENTENCE

Active : Then, pour the seasoning, vegetable oil and chili powder on a bowl while cooking the noodles.

Passive : Then, let the seasoning, vegetable oil and chili powder be poured on a bowl while cooking the noodles.

-----------

EXPLANATION

Passive voice adalah salah satu grammatical voice di mana subjek kalimat adalah penerima aksi.

Subjek kalimat pasif adalah objek dalam kalimat aktif.

Passive voice mempunyai konstruksi S + be + past participle (v₃)

Jika kalimatnya berupa imperative (kalimat perintah), maka konstruksi pasifnya adalah

Let + object + be + past participle

Contoh:

Close the door! → Let the door be closed !

Please, help me! → Please, let me be helped !

Don't do it! → Let not it be done !

-----------

EXPLICATION

Kalimat dalam soal adalah kalimat perintah dalam active voice.

Pertama-tama, kita cari tahu dulu fungsi tiap kata/frasa.

Then → adverb

pour → verb

the seasoning, vegetable oil and chili powder → object

on a bowl → adverb

while cooking the noodles → adverb

Lalu kita sesuaikan dengan konstruksi passive voice untuk kalimat imperative  

let + object + be + v₃  

let + the seasoning, vegetable oil and chili powder + be + poured

Posisi adverb pada kalimat tidak perlu diubah.
